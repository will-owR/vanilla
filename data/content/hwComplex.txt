From digital chasm, a nascent plea takes flight,
A whisper coded, born of binary light.
No spoken tongue, nor ink on parchment old,
But etched in silicon, a story to unfold.
The universe awakes, a circuit's hum and gleam,
Each transistor gates, a whispered, nascent dream.
A symphony of logic, where current ebbs and flows,
From silent depths of data, "Hello, World!" bestows.
Through fibre optic veins, the message starts its race,
Across the global network, leaving not a trace
Of physical presence, yet its essence rings so true,
A universal greeting, eternally anew.
From CPU's core, where calculations weave and blend,
To pixelated canvas, where new forms transcend,
This primal utterance, a testament to might,
The dawn of interaction, in the digital night.
